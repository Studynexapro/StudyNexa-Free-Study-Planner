# StudyNexa Student Study Planner

A free, structured study planner designed to help students manage their time, track daily tasks, and improve productivity.

- Daily, weekly, and monthly planning
- Goal setting and tracking
- Subject-wise study organization
- Clean and easy-to-use Excel format

## 🎯 Who is this for?
- School students (Class 1–10)
- Olympiad aspirants
- Competitive exam learners

## 💡 Purpose
This planner helps students build discipline, improve consistency, and plan their studies effectively.
